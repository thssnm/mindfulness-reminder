A minimalistic app for more mindfulness in everyday life.

<b>Features:</b>

- Periodic reminders with varying mindfulness prompts
- Daily rotating inspirational texts
- Minimalistic, calm design
- Fully open source
- No trackers, no analytics

The app sends a simple question at regular intervals: "Where are you right now?" - an invitation to pause briefly and be present in the moment.

Notifications only occur during waking hours (8am-10pm) and can be activated or deactivated at any time.
